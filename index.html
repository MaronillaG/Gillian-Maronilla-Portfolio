Get To Know Me.

Last year I decided to take a step away from working in Finance after 7 years to travel and explore my interest in tech. I want to pursue a new career that offers a lot more freedom in my day to day, think creatively towards problem solving within a team that is commited to learning.

I had a taste of computer science during an intense 4 week bootcamp. I learned some fundamentals of programming, learned a bit of C programming language, working with a terminal, GIT and also collaborating with others. I enjoyed the experience immensely and have made some life long friends but I felt that I still needed to explore other options.

In January 2023 I found freeCodeCamp and completed the Responsive Web Design certification and something clicked. I really enjoyed being able to physically see what I was building as I was building it and I enjoyed making. I knew I wanted to start making magic.
